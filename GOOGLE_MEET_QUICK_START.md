## 🚀 How to Start a Google Meet Call

### In 3 Simple Steps:

**Step 1: Select Contact**
- Click on a contact in your chat list
- Chat opens

**Step 2: Click Video Icon**
- Look at chat header (top-right)
- Find 📹 video icon
- Click it

**Step 3: Start Meeting**
- Click "Start Google Meet Call"
- Google Meet opens in new tab
- Video call active! 🎥

## 📤 Share Meeting Link

### After Meeting Starts:
```
✅ Meeting Started!

Meeting Link:
https://meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij

[Copy] [Share in Chat] [Open] [End]
```

### Options:
1. **Copy** - Copy link to clipboard
2. **Share in Chat** - Send link to contact
3. **Open** - Open Google Meet again
4. **End** - End the meeting

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Video Icon Not Showing?
- Make sure contact is selected
- Refresh page
- Try different contact

### Google Meet Not Opening?
- Check if pop-ups blocked
- Allow pop-ups for this site
- Try different browser

### Can't Copy Link?
- Try again
- Check clipboard permissions
- Use manual copy
